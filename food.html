Brain Tumor Diet To Control Symptoms

For nausea and vomiting:

1.Limit foods with strong odours.
2.Eat small and frequent meals.
3.Consume bland foods (potatoes, cheese, hard boiled eggs, curd, etc).
4.Avoid overly spicy, greasy or sweetened foods and fluids.
5.Try ginger tea or ginger water, etc.
6.Stay upright >30 mins after eating

For diarrhea:

1.Eat small meals, which are often are easiest to tolerate.
2.Try BRATY diet (Bananas, Rice, Applesauce, Toast, Yogurt).
3.Eat bland foods - avoid greasy, fatty or fried foods.
4.Drink plenty of fluids (non-carbonated and caffeine-free).

For constipation:

1.Drink plenty of fluids, try prune juice (noncarbonated), or drink a warm beverage.
2.Adopt bowel regimen and add some activity to you routine.
3.Gradually add fibre to diet - as tolerated.
4.If bloated, avoid gas forming foods such as beans, broccoli, cauliflower, onions, garlic, etc.

For fatigue:

1.Use of oral nutritional shakes may be of benefit Sore mouth.
2.Cool or room-temperature foods often easier to tolerate than warm.
3.Use soft, tender foods.
4.Use a straw.
5.Eat non-acidic foods and fluids.
6.Avoid alcohol-containing mouth rinses but practice good oral hygiene. May consider use of mouth numbing rinses prior to eating.
